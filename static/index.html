About

Isn't it a pure luck to have your profession as your hobby?

Mare Hunt

One could call me a serial learner. That's the reason I love the web: one has to learn constantly to keep up. It‘s still a miracle to me that you write a couple of lines with the right syntax and things start to happen. I hope to never lose my sense of wonder. They say; curiosity killed the cat, but I say: curiosity keeps you going.

Yesterday I was creating ads. Today I am building websites. Tomorrow I hope to be building clean, accessible, simple and intuitive applications.

And it all started with the Zoo. My first job was to feed wild wolves and bears.
